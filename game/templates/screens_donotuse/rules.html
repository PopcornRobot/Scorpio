A MURDER WALKS IN...

An hour ago, there was a murder down the street. The suspect fled on foot and hid amongst our group here. Little did they know they hid in a party of seasoned detectives. Police are relying on us to find them.

HOW THE GAME WORKS

The game is similar to Mafia. Our goal is to find the criminal hidden in our group. At the end of the game, we vote on who is the murderer.



To help our search, we rely on tips that reveal information about the murderer. Real facts like “this person skydived" that lead to real people. It’s your job to talk to each other and find out who the murderer is.

ROLES

Detective

  1. Interrogate everyone.
  2. Find murderer.
  3. Trust no one.

Informant


  1. Deliver tip to trusted detectives.
  2. The murderer is hunting you. Keep your identity a secret to stay alive.

Criminal

  1. Convince everyone you are not the murderer.
  2. Kill informants before they spread information about you.

.

3 Cardinal Rules

  1. No lies. If someone asks you a question about your life, tell them the truth (within reasonable bounds, of course).

    You can lie about who you think is the murderer.

  2. Check phone every 10 minutes. Your screen updates with new objectives and important information as the game progresses.

  3. Hide your screen! Don't compromise your identity. Your screen reveals information about your identity.
