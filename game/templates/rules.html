A murderer walks in...

Ladies and gentlemen, an hour ago, there was a murder down the street. The suspect fled on foot and hid amongst our group here. Little did they know they hid in a party of seasoned detectives. Police are relying on us to find them.

 

How the game works

This game is like a modified game of Mafia. Our goal is to find the criminal. At the end of the game, we vote on who the murderer is.

To help our search, we rely on tips that reveal facts about the murderer. These facts are real, like “this person skydived" that lead to real people. It’s your job to talk to each other and find out who the murderer is.

 

Roles

Detective

  1. Interrogate everyone.
  2. Find murderer.
  3. Trust no one.

Informant

  1. Secretely deliver tip to detectives.
  2. Stay alive. The murderer is hunting you. Keep your identity secret.
  3. Trust no one.

Murderer

  1. Convince everyone you are not the murderer.
  2. Hunt informants and kill them. They are trying to spread information about you.

 

 

2 Cardinal Rules

  1. No lies. If someone asks you a question about your life, tell them the truth (within reasonable bounds, of course).

    You can lie about who you think is the murderer.

  2. Check your phone. Your screen updates as the game progresses. It reveals new objectives and important information throughout the game. Check your screen every 10 minutes!

    Hide your screen!
