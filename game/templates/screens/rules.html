SCORPIO STRIKES AGAIN


Evidence linking the mysterious criminal Scorpio to the Galway murders went missing last Sunday.

Internal Affairs is investigating detectives for possible links to the nefarious criminal.


HOW THE GAME WORKS

Detectives have reason to believe Scorpio will infiltrate tonight's party. Your goal is to reveal the identity of the criminal Scorpio.

Tips about Scorpio’s identity will be dropped throughout the game. Tips that are real facts (e.g. “this person skydived") that lead to real people.

At the end of the game, the group will vote on who is Scorpio.


ROLES

Detective

  1. Interrogate everyone to find Scorpio.
  2. Spread tips.
  3. Trust no one.

Scorpio

  1. Convince everyone you are a detective, not the criminal Scorpio.
  2. Prevent the spread of information about you.

THREE CARDINAL RULES

  1. No lies. If someone asks you a question about your life, tell them the truth (within reasonable bounds, of course).

    You can lie about who you think is the murderer.

  2. Keep this page open. Don't close this page. This page automatically updates as the game progresses.

  3. Check your phone every 10 minutes. This page updates with new objectives and important information.

    Hide your screen or you will compromise your identity!


PLEASE WAIT HERE

When the game starts, this page will automatically update. Leave your phone on this page and wait for instructions.
