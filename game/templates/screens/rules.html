SCORPIO STRIKES AGAIN


Evidence linking the mysterious criminal Scorpio to the Galway murders went missing last Sunday.

Internal Affairs is investigating detectives for possible links to the nefarious criminal.


HOW THE GAME WORKS

Detectives have reason to believe Scorpio will infiltrate tonight's party. Your goal is to gather notes and find the criminal.

Tips about Scorpio’s identity will be dropped throughout the game. Tips that are real facts (e.g. “this person skydived") that lead to real people.

At the end of the game, the group will vote on who is Scorpio.


ROLES

Detective

  1. Interrogate everyone to find Scorpio.
  2. Spread tips.
  3. Trust no one.

Scorpio

  1. Convince everyone you are a detective.
  2. Kill detectives before they spread information about you.

THREE CARDINAL RULES

  1. No lies. If someone asks you a question about your life, tell them the truth (within reason, of course).

    You can lie about who you think is the murderer.

  2. Check your phone every 10 minutes. Your screen updates with new objectives and important information as the game progresses.

  3. Hide your screen! Your screen reveals your mission and identity.
